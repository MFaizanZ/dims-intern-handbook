Meet CNS & the 6 Projects

You now know what DIMS is. This page is a quick tour of the company building it — CNS — and a one-screen map of the six projects you’ll keep hearing about. Chapter 17 covers them in full; this is the orientation.

Who CNS is

CNS (㈜시엔에스) is a Korean company that builds DIMS — a Drone Inspection Management System — and runs it across six funded projects. The company’s hardware foundation is DJI enterprise gear: the Dock 3 + Matrice 4TD you met in chapter 01. The strategy is simple to state: build one strong platform, then prove and sell it across many real inspection jobs.

🧩 Analogy

Think of CNS like a company that builds one excellent game engine (DIMS) and then ships several different games on top of it (forest-fire monitoring, tower-crane inspection, …). The engine is the real asset; each “game” proves it works and pays for the next one.

🧭 Where you fit

As interns you’ll mostly touch the platform core — the software (chapters 12–14) and the AI/data (chapter 15, plus the Data Voucher dataset). Knowing how your work feeds the field deployments (especially the Bomunsan forest-fire launch) is what makes the day-to-day make sense.
